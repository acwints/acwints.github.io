What’s Around the Corner for Medium

Substack has been getting so much attention recently so it's not surprising to see Medium trying to step their game up. Medium has always been a bit of a disappointment in the public eye, but, as is often the case, the public might be wrong and it looks like they still have a talented team heads down on product and ignoring the noise.

Article
NYC Is Dead Forever

I don't know if I'm as pessimistic as James about the future of NYC, but I would agree that I don't think it will ever be the same. COVID-19 is peeling back all of our assumptions about the importance of city life and as much as I love NYC, it's going to be difficult to justify the shoebox apartments and expensive everything when the cute restaurants and social experiences just aren't what they used to be.
